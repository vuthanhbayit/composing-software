The Dao of Immutability (The Way of
the Functional Programmer)
Functional programming is a foundational pillar of JavaScript, and immutability is a foundational
pillar of functional programming. You can’t fully understand functional programming without first
understanding immutability. This story may help.
Forward

computation. There I found a scroll that seemed to have fallen on the floor, forgotten.

Lambda.”
