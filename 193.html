Early Termination
It’s possible to signal to other transducers in the pipeline that we’re done reducing, and they should
not expect to process any more values. Upon seeing a
reduced
value, other transducers may decide to
stop adding to the collection, and the transducing process (as controlled by the final
step()

may decide to stop enumerating over values. The transducing process may make one more call as
