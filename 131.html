Linked lists form the basis of lots of other data structures and aggregations, such as arrays, strings,
and various kinds of trees. The DOM tree is based on linked lists, with DOM nodes pointing to
children, children nodes pointing back to parents, and delegated references to sibling nodes. There
are many other possible kinds of aggregation. We won’t cover them all in-depth here.
